# First 90 Days

## Days 1–14: Establish truth

- Name human owners for growth, clinical review, privacy, brand, budget and systems.
- Capture trailing 12-month revenue and the KPI baseline.
- Document services, pricing, direct costs, provider/device capacity and active promotions.
- Map every lead source and the full inquiry-to-returning-patient journey.
- Audit website conversion paths, local search presence, social performance, reviews and CRM follow-up.
- Define approved brand voice, visual assets, claims and consent inventory.
- Select integrations using least privilege; never import PHI into GitHub.

**Deliverable:** verified baseline, bottleneck map and ranked opportunity register.

## Days 15–30: Repair conversion leaks

- Set a measurable response-time standard.
- Standardize warm lead response, qualification, booking, reminder, no-show recovery and consultation follow-up.
- Repair mismatched calls to action and landing pages.
- Establish source tracking and booked-consult attribution.
- Build the first monthly content production day.

**Deliverable:** conversion playbook and clean measurement.

## Days 31–60: Launch three controlled experiments

Recommended starting hypotheses, subject to baseline:

1. Provider-led menopause/HRT authority series with a dedicated consultation path.
2. Skin-correction campaign pairing education with Hollywood Spectra or the most suitable available-capacity service.
3. Ethical reactivation campaign for eligible lapsed patients using approved, privacy-compliant systems.

Each experiment needs economics, audience, content, landing path, follow-up, owner, approval and stop rule.

**Deliverable:** experiment reports based on booked and collected outcomes.

## Days 61–90: Scale winners and build retention

- Scale only experiments meeting conversion, margin and experience thresholds.
- Launch structured rebooking and maintenance education.
- Improve membership explanation and fit assessment.
- Establish ethical review and referral moments.
- Create the next-quarter service-line and capacity plan.
- Retire low-performing campaigns and document learning.

**Deliverable:** next-quarter plan with forecast ranges, resource requirements and risks.

## 90-day success criteria

- Reliable baseline and source attribution.
- Visible lead response, booking, show and treatment conversion.
- At least one repeatable profitable acquisition engine.
- At least one measurable retention improvement.
- Weekly executive reporting in use.
- No unresolved privacy or clinical-governance gaps.
