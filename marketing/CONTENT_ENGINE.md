# Premium Viral Content Engine

## Objective

Create culturally relevant, expert-led content that makes Illumé memorable and turns attention into qualified local appointments—without sacrificing trust, taste or clinical accuracy.

## Content pillars

1. **Expert clarity:** providers answer the questions patients are embarrassed or confused to ask.
2. **Visible transformation:** consented journeys, process education and realistic timelines.
3. **Myth vs reality:** respectful correction of trending misinformation.
4. **Inside Illumé:** premium environment, people, devices, standards and thoughtful details.
5. **Patient identity:** confidence, energy, aging well and feeling like oneself.
6. **Local relevance:** Lee's Summit/KC seasons, events, routines and community partners.
7. **Proof of care:** credentials, consultation process, safety, personalization and follow-through.

## The VELVET creative filter

Every concept is scored 1–5:

- **V**isual stopping power
- **E**motional recognition
- **L**ocal relevance
- **V**erifiable expertise
- **E**legant brand fit
- **T**rackable next action

Do not produce concepts scoring below 22/30 without revision.

## Weekly production rhythm

- 2 authority videos
- 1 transformation/process story where compliant consent exists
- 1 personality or behind-the-scenes piece
- 1 conversion-focused offer/service explanation
- Daily lightweight Stories built from the week's production
- 1 email and 1 Google Business post repurposed from the strongest idea
- 1 SEO article or meaningful update based on high-intent demand

Cadence is a starting hypothesis; performance and staff capacity decide the final schedule.

## Hook patterns

- “If you are doing everything right but still experiencing ___, start here.”
- “What I wish every patient knew before booking ___.”
- “Three reasons your previous ___ may not have worked.”
- “The difference between looking treated and looking refreshed.”
- “A provider explains the trend everyone is asking about.”
- “Who is—and is not—a good candidate for ___.”

Avoid guaranteed outcomes, shame, exaggerated fear and generic “limited spots” language.

## Viral-to-booking path

Each post must have one primary next step: save, share, comment a question, view a service guide, request a consultation or reply by approved channel. The landing destination must match the promise of the content.

## Test matrix

Test one variable at a time:
- Hook
- First-frame visual
- Presenter
- Length
- Proof element
- Call to action
- Landing page

Measure 3-second hold, average watch percentage, saves/shares, qualified local engagement, profile/landing visits, qualified leads, booked consults and acquired patients.

## Premium visual direction

- Black, warm ivory/cream, restrained gold, charcoal and selected soft neutrals.
- Clean composition, intentional negative space, natural skin texture and flattering editorial light.
- Avoid over-retouching, cluttered price graphics, generic AI faces, excessive fonts and constant sale styling.
- Use real Illumé providers, environment and equipment wherever possible.
- AI imagery must be clearly controlled, reviewed for anatomical artifacts and never presented as a real patient result.

## Trend-response SLA

Spark logs a relevant trend, Lens verifies momentum and context, Muse adapts it to the brand, Sentinel reviews claims, and a human approves publishing. Fast never means careless.
