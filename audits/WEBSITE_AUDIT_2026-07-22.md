# Illumé Website Audit — 2026-07-22

Scope: Public, read-only review of illumemedspa.com across technical SEO, conversion, patient journey, content quality, brand consistency, accessibility signals and medical-marketing risk.

## Executive conclusion

The site is crawlable and has strong local basics, a modern homepage and several strong new 2026 articles. The largest risk is legacy content and inconsistent conversion architecture—not a broken website. Fix credibility, claims, duplicate pages and booking friction before buying substantially more traffic.
