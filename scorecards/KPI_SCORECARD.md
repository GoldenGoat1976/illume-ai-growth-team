## Required breakdowns

Segment, where data quality permits, by service line, provider, new vs returning patient, channel and campaign. Never expose patient identities in this repository.

## Weekly traffic lights

- **Green:** on or above target with acceptable quality/margin.
- **Yellow:** within 10% of target or data is incomplete.
- **Red:** more than 10% below target, material quality issue or negative trend for two periods.

Every red metric needs one accountable action, an owner and a due date. Avoid inventing explanations when attribution is unknown.
