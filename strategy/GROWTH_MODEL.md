# Path to 20%+ Growth

## Revenue model

Collected revenue can be managed through six levers:

**Qualified demand × booking rate × show rate × treatment conversion × average collected revenue × repeat frequency**, constrained by provider/device/room capacity.

The team should not assume every lever must rise 20%. Compounding modest gains can exceed the target. Example planning scenario:

| Lever | Illustrative improvement |
|---|---:|
| Qualified leads | +7% |
| Lead-to-booked-consult rate | +5% |
| Show rate | +3% |
| Consult-to-treatment conversion | +4% |
| Repeat/retention contribution | +3% |

These are planning assumptions, not promises. Replace them with Illumé's baseline and capacity constraints.

## Strategic growth pillars

1. **Own premium local authority:** menopause/HRT, medical weight management, skin correction, laser expertise and longevity.
2. **Convert existing demand better:** response time, scripts, booking friction, reminders, consultation quality and structured follow-up.
3. **Increase lifetime value responsibly:** treatment plans, maintenance, memberships, cross-service education, rebooking and win-back.
4. **Fill profitable capacity:** target underused provider/device time without training customers to wait for discounts.
5. **Build referral gravity:** remarkable experience, ethical review requests, patient education and aligned local partners.
6. **Measure profit and quality:** collected revenue, contribution margin, complaints, refunds and clinical escalation—not revenue alone.

## Portfolio allocation

- 60% proven channels and offers.
- 25% iterative improvements.
- 15% high-upside experiments.

Run no more than three material experiments simultaneously. Each experiment needs a single owner, baseline, target, deadline, budget ceiling and stop rule.

## Quarterly planning questions

- Where is the largest verified revenue leak?
- Which service has demand, margin and available capacity?
- Which audience problem can Illumé credibly own?
- What would improve conversion without discounting?
- Which retention moment is currently missing?
- What should be stopped to protect focus?
