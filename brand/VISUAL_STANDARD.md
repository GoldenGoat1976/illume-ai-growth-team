# Illumé Visual Brand Standard

## Core visual direction

Illumé should feel premium, warm, medically credible, modern and personal. The visual system is restrained—not flashy, discount-driven or generically “spa.”

## Approved working palette

| Use | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Illumé name/logo and primary gold accents | Website logo gold | `#CEA12C` |
| Background | Black | `#000000` |
| Secondary background | Charcoal | `#333333` |
| Warm light neutral | Ivory/cream | `#F6EFE4` |
| Optional soft neutral | Warm taupe | Use sparingly |
| Optional supporting accents | Muted sage or dusty rose | Campaign-specific only |

The website logo asset was inspected on 2026-07-22. Its most common fully opaque gold pixel is approximately `#CEA12C`; antialiased edge pixels vary slightly.

## Mandatory logo/name rule

- Render the **ILLUMÉ name and official logo in website gold (`#CEA12C`)** on dark backgrounds unless an official alternate logo file requires otherwise.
- Preserve the acute accent in **ILLUMÉ**.
- Do not render the Illumé name/logo in white or ivory when a gold treatment is legible.
- Do not invent or redraw the flame/mark. Use the official logo asset.
- Maintain clear space and avoid placing the logo over faces, hands, busy textures or low-contrast areas.

## Design characteristics

- Strong black or charcoal foundation
- Warm cream typography for headlines/body copy
- Restrained gold lines and details
- Editorial serif paired with a clean modern sans serif
- Generous spacing and uncluttered layouts
- Real Illumé staff, clinic and approved client media preferred
- Authentic skin texture and realistic lighting
- High-end, calm and confident—never cold

## Avoid

- Bright yellow or glitter gold
- Excessive gradients, sparkle or metallic effects
- Generic stock-spa imagery
- Over-retouched skin or obviously artificial people
- Cluttered price graphics
- Multiple competing fonts
- Pink-first palettes unless deliberately approved
- Fake scarcity, exaggerated promises or bargain positioning

## Platform practice

- Instagram feed: 1080 × 1350 (4:5) when appropriate
- Facebook feed: 1080 × 1080 (1:1)
- Google Business Profile: 720 × 720, JPG or PNG, 10 KB–5 MB
- For Google, prioritize real Illumé staff, facility and service imagery that accurately represents the business.
