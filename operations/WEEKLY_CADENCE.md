# Operating Cadence

## Daily asynchronous cycle

1. Conductor checks scorecard exceptions, deadlines and approvals.
2. Specialists complete research, analysis and drafts.
3. Sentinel reviews medical/privacy risk where applicable.
4. Conductor packages decisions into a short approval queue.
5. Humans approve, revise or reject consequential actions.
6. Results and learning are logged.

## Monday Growth Council — 30 minutes

- Five-minute scorecard: revenue, qualified demand, conversion, retention, capacity and margin.
- Review last week's experiments and actual results.
- Choose the three highest-value actions for the week.
- Confirm owners, approval needs and deadlines.
- Explicitly stop or defer lower-value work.

## Wednesday Creative Review — 20 minutes

- Review next week's campaign and content slate.
- Check VELVET scores, clinical accuracy, brand fit and conversion path.
- Approve production—not publication—unless designated approvers explicitly approve both.

## Friday Learning Review — 20 minutes

- What shipped?
- What moved?
- What did not?
- What did we learn?
- What will change next week?
- Are any metrics being gamed or creating poor patient experience?

## Monthly owner review — 60 minutes

Review profit, service-line performance, retention cohorts, paid-channel acquisition, staffing/capacity, reputation, compliance issues and the next month's experiment portfolio.

## Work-item format

Every task contains: outcome, metric, owner, due date, inputs, dependencies, approval tier, acceptance criteria and next review date.
