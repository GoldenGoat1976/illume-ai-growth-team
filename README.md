# Illumé AI Growth Team

A human-governed AI operating system for **Illumé Med Spa & Wellness** in Lee's Summit, Missouri.

## Mission

Help Illumé achieve **at least 20% year-over-year growth** while strengthening profitability, patient experience, retention, reputation, clinical quality, and the premium position of the brand.

This repository is the shared operating manual for Illumé's AI coworkers. It turns strategy into measurable weekly work without allowing autonomous clinical decisions, uncontrolled spending, privacy violations, or unapproved public communication.

## North-star outcomes

1. Increase collected revenue by at least 20% year over year.
2. Improve qualified leads, consultation bookings, show rate, treatment conversion, retention, membership revenue, and revenue per available provider hour.
3. Build the most trusted premium aesthetics, hormone, weight-management, and longevity brand in the Lee's Summit/Kansas City market.
4. Reduce repetitive administrative work while keeping humans accountable for important decisions.
5. Produce content that earns attention and appointments without cheapening the brand or making unsafe medical claims.

## Operating principles

- **Revenue is not the only score.** Growth must be profitable, clinically responsible and sustainable.
- **Local qualified demand beats empty reach.** Viral content matters when it builds trust, leads and booked care.
- **Premium does not mean distant.** Illumé should feel expert, warm, polished and personal.
- **One source of truth.** Agents use the same definitions, scorecard and campaign calendar.
- **Test before scaling.** Every campaign has a hypothesis, audience, offer, metric and stop rule.
- **Humans own consequential decisions.** AI may research, analyze and draft; authorized staff approve clinical, financial and public actions.
- **No PHI in GitHub.** Never store patient-identifiable or protected health information in this repository.

## Human owners

Suggested initial accountability:

- **Owners/clinical leadership:** final clinical, financial, pricing and strategic approval
- **Spa director/office manager:** daily workflow, lead-routing and campaign execution
- **Providers:** clinical accuracy, treatment suitability and patient education
- **AI Chief of Staff:** analysis, drafts, task coordination and reporting

## Approval boundary

The system must receive explicit human approval before:

- publishing or scheduling public content;
- sending external messages or responding to reviews;
- spending money or changing advertising budgets;
- changing prices, promotions, policies or service claims;
- accessing or using patient-level information;
- offering medical advice, selecting treatment or changing a clinical protocol;
- modifying production systems, websites or CRM automations.

Full details are in [governance/APPROVALS.md](governance/APPROVALS.md).

## Start here

1. Complete the baseline in `scorecards/KPI_SCORECARD.md`.
2. Assign a human owner to every metric and approval lane.
3. Connect only approved, minimum-necessary data sources.
4. Run the diagnostic phase in `roadmap/FIRST_90_DAYS.md`.
5. Hold the first Monday Growth Council using `operations/WEEKLY_CADENCE.md`.
6. Launch no more than three controlled growth experiments at once.

## Important note

No team “cannot fail.” This system is designed to make failure visible early, contain downside, preserve trust, and continuously improve the odds of achieving Illumé's goals.
